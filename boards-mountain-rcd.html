Mountain Resource Conservation District

The Mountain RCD works to maintain environmental quality and increase community development opportunities, assisting with identification, planning, and implementation of natural resource–related projects across twelve West Virginia counties, including Mercer.

The Mountain RCD exists to maintain environmental quality and increase community development opportunities. We assist with identification, planning, and implementing natural resource–related projects. Volunteer with the Mountain RCD to become more active in your community.

West Virginia Counties served: Braxton, Fayette, Greenbrier, McDowell, Mercer, Monroe, Nicholas, Pocahontas, Raleigh, Summers, Webster, and Wyoming.
