Boards & Authorities

Boards, authorities & commissions.

Citizen boards and public authorities carry out specialized work for Mercer County. Many have seats open to resident volunteers.

💧

Public Service Districts

Bluewell, Bramwell, Green Valley–Glenwood, Lashmeet and Oakvale PSDs providing water & sewer service.

✈️

Airport Authority

Governs operations and development of the Mercer County Airport.

♻️

Solid Waste Authority

Oversees solid-waste planning, recycling and litter programs.

🗺️

Planning Commission

Land-use planning, subdivision review and long-range county planning.

🏥

Board of Health

Governs the county Health Department and public-health policy.

📟

911 Advisory Board

Advises on emergency-communications operations and addressing.

🔥

Fire Board

Coordinates and supports the county's volunteer fire departments.

🏗️

Building Commission

Holds and finances county-owned buildings and facilities.

🏛️

Civil Service Commission

Administers civil-service processes for eligible county employees.

📈

Development Authority Board

Directs economic-development strategy and incentives.

🧳

CVB Board

Governs the Convention & Visitors Bureau and tourism marketing.

🏚️

Dilapidated Structures Committee

Reviews and prioritizes unsafe-structure abatement cases.

🏞️

Glenwood Park Board

Oversees Glenwood Recreational Park facilities.

🛡️

Public Defender Board

Supports indigent-defense services in the county.

🌐

Region One Planning & Development

Regional development council serving southern West Virginia counties.

🛣️

King Coal & Coal Heritage Highway Authorities

Advance major regional highway corridors and heritage tourism.

🤝

Southern Regional Community Corrections

Regional alternative-sentencing and reentry programming.

👷

WV Workforce Board

Workforce development and job-training coordination.
