Boards, Authorities & Commissions

The County Commission appoints citizen members to the boards and authorities that guide public services. Interested in serving? Submit a letter of interest.

Bluewell Public Service District Board

The Bluewell Public Service District Board governs the public service district serving the Bluewell area of Mercer County. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Bramwell Public Service District Board

The Bramwell Public Service District Board governs the public service district serving the Bramwell area of Mercer County. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

Civil Service Commission

The Civil Service Commission administers civil-service processes for eligible Mercer County employees. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

Community Action of Southeastern WV (CASEWV)

CASEWV, Inc. is a community action agency chartered in 1964—the third such agency established in West Virginia—dedicated to empowering individuals and families in southeastern West Virginia to achieve self-sufficiency and improve their quality of life.

Details →

Glenwood Park Board

The Glenwood Park Board oversees Glenwood Recreational Park facilities. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

Green Valley-Glenwood Public Service District

Green Valley-Glenwood PSD, founded in 1963, provides quality and affordable water, sewer, and sprinkler services to residential and commercial customers in Mercer County.

Details →

Hatfield Trail

The Hatfield Trail board supports access to the Hatfield-McCoy Trail system in Mercer County. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

King Coal Highway Authority

Established by the West Virginia Legislature in March 1999, the King Coal Highway Authority promotes and advances the construction of a modern highway through McDowell, Mercer, Mingo, Wyoming, and Wayne counties along US Route 52.

Details →

Lashmeet Public Service District

The Lashmeet Public Service District provides water/sewer service in the Lashmeet area of Mercer County. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

Mercer County Airport Authority

The Mercer County Airport Authority is the governing board for the county's airport operations and facilities. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

Mercer County Board of Health

The Mercer County Board of Health is the governing body overseeing public health policy and the county Health Department. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

Mercer County Building Commission

The Mercer County Building Commission holds and finances county-owned buildings and facilities on behalf of the Mercer County Commission. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

Mercer County Convention and Visitors Bureau Board

The Mercer County Convention and Visitors Bureau is a non-profit destination marketing organization and the official tourism marketing body for Mercer County, funded by the six percent lodging tax paid by visitors.

Details →

Mercer County Development Authority

The Mercer County Development Authority promotes business investment in Mercer County, which has a history in manufacturing, technology, and tourism and is served by I-77 with access to eight major regional markets.

Details →

Mercer County Dilapidated Structures Committee

The Mercer County Dilapidated Structures Committee reviews and prioritizes abatement of abandoned and deteriorating structures under the county's dilapidated buildings ordinance. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

Mercer County E-911 Advisory Board

The Mercer County E-911 Advisory Board advises on the county's emergency 911 communications system and addressing. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

Mercer County Fire Board

The Mercer County Fire Board coordinates and supports fire protection services and the county's volunteer fire departments. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

Mercer County Planning Commission

The Mercer County Planning Commission develops and implements comprehensive strategies to foster economic, cultural, and community growth throughout Mercer County, WV, guided by members uniquely qualified to assess local conditions and plan for the future.

Details →

Mercer County Solid Waste Authority

The Mercer County Solid Waste Authority (MCSWA) is governed by five volunteer board members appointed to four-year terms under WV Code 22C-4-3, meeting monthly on the 4th Monday.

Details →

Mountain Resource Conservation District

The Mountain RCD works to maintain environmental quality and increase community development opportunities, assisting with identification, planning, and implementation of natural resource–related projects across twelve West Virginia counties, including Mercer.

Details →

National Coal Heritage Highway Authority Board

The National Coal Heritage Highway Authority Board is the governing body for the National Coal Heritage Highway in the Mercer County region. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

Oakvale Public Service District

The Oakvale Public Service District provides water/sewer service in the Oakvale area of Mercer County. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

Public Defender Board

The Public Defender Board supports indigent-defense services in Mercer County. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

Region I Planning & Development Council

Region I is a public agency and one of eleven Regional Planning & Development Councils in West Virginia, providing comprehensive planning and development services across six counties and twenty-seven municipalities in southern West Virginia.

Details →

Southern Regional Community Corrections

Southern Regional Community Corrections provides regional alternative-sentencing and reentry programming. Details are maintained on the official county site.

Details →

WV Workforce

WV Workforce serves Mercer County residents from its Princeton, WV office location at 195 Davis St., Suite 102.
