About the County

A shining community in Appalachia.

Mercer County is more than a place on a map — it's where families thrive, dreams take flight, and a vibrant community spirit flourishes.

A warm welcome & a shared vision

From the Commission

As elected officials, we are honored to serve the people of Mercer County with integrity, transparency, and unwavering dedication. We welcome visitors with open arms and invite you to experience the genuine warmth and hospitality of our community.

Join us as we build a future where opportunity meets tradition, and where the Appalachian spirit inspires us to reach new heights. Welcome to Mercer County — your next chapter begins here.

Our towns

Communities of Mercer County.

Princeton

County seat and home to the courthouse and the Mercer Street arts district.

Bluefield

The "City of Bluefield" on the Virginia line, gateway to East River Mountain.

Bramwell

Historic coal-baron town — once home to more millionaires per capita than anywhere in America.

Athens

College town and home to Concord University.

Oakvale

Small incorporated town in eastern Mercer County.

Glenwood

Community anchored by Glenwood Recreational Park.

Lashmeet & Matoaka

Rural communities across the county's northern reaches.

Montcalm & more

Unincorporated communities that make up the fabric of the county.
