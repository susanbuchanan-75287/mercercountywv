Emergency Information

In an emergency, dial 911.

Mercer County 911 and Emergency Management coordinate the county's response to fires, medical emergencies, severe weather and disasters.

Call 911
Key contacts

Who to call.

🚑

Life-threatening emergency

Fire, medical or police emergency — dial 911 immediately.

📟

Mercer County 911

Emergency communications & dispatch. Administrative questions (addressing, non-emergency) go through the 911 office.

🚨

Office of Emergency Management

Disaster preparedness and response coordination for the county.

🚔

Sheriff's Department

Non-emergency law-enforcement matters — contact via the courthouse office line (304) 487-8306.

Be prepared

Preparedness tips.

Make a plan

Know your evacuation routes, meeting points, and how your family will communicate if separated.

Build a kit

Water, non-perishable food, medications, flashlight, batteries, and important documents for at least 3 days.

Stay informed

Monitor NWS alerts and local media during severe weather. Sign up for any available county alert system.
