Mercer County 911

The Mercer Communications Center is the Public Safety Answering & Dispatch Point for police, fire, EMS and emergency management countywide — processing 911 and non-emergency calls promptly and professionally to help save lives and protect property.

Emergency Management (OES)

Disaster preparedness, response coordination and public-safety planning for Mercer County.

Animal Shelter

An open-admission county shelter (Director Stacy Harman) providing humane care for stray and relinquished animals. Accepts animals from county residents; all pets are spayed/neutered before adoption; rescue-friendly with 501(c)(3) partners.

Health Department

Empowers residents to live healthy lives in healthy communities. Full clinic services via Blue Prince Family Health: primary and preventative care, wellness and cancer screenings, immunizations and vaccines.

Mercer County Airport

General-aviation airport (Manager Jim Pilkins) serving the greater Bluefield/Princeton region under the oversight of the County Commission.

Recycling Program

Drop-off recycling at the Sanitary Landfill. Hours: Mon–Fri 7 AM–5 PM, Sat 8 AM–1 PM. Accepts #1 & #2 plastics, ferrous metals & cans, all aluminum, and bagged paper / broken-down cardboard (materials must be separated).

Day Report Center

Community-based alternative sentencing and supervision programs.

Corrections / Regional Jail

Coordination with the Southern Regional Jail and inmate services.

Adult Probation

Supervision of adults on probation within the judicial circuit.
