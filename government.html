Government

The Mercer County Commission

The Commission is the elected governing body of the county — adopting the budget, levying taxes, overseeing agencies and appointing boards.

BA

Bill Archer

Commission President

Current — signs the Commission's 2026 official notices as President

Commissioner Bill Archer serves as President of the Mercer County Commission for 2026. A longtime advocate for the county, he presides over commission meetings and represents residents across Mercer County.

GP

Greg Puckett

Commissioner (Region III)

Terms: 2015–2020, 2021–2026

A native southern West Virginian and alumnus of Princeton Senior High and Concord College, Greg Puckett is Executive Director of Community Connections Inc. He helped re-establish the County Planning Commission, launched the award-winning Keep Mercer Clean campaign, invested nearly $2M in the county park, and paid off the regional-jail debt. He serves on the NACo Board of Directors and earned the National Public Leadership in the Arts Award (2021) and the Louis Gorin Award for rural health (2020).

BB

Brian Blankenship

Commissioner

Current term

Commissioner Brian Blankenship serves on the Mercer County Commission, focusing on infrastructure, public safety and responsible stewardship of county resources on behalf of all residents.

What we do

Responsibilities

  • Adopt the annual county budget and set the property-tax levy
  • Oversee county departments, agencies and facilities
  • Appoint members to boards, authorities and commissions
  • Sit as the Board of Review & Equalization on assessments
  • Adopt county ordinances and enter into contracts
  • Maintain public records and conduct open public meetings
