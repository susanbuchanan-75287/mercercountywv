Community Action of Southeastern WV (CASEWV)

CASEWV, Inc. is a community action agency chartered in 1964—the third such agency established in West Virginia—dedicated to empowering individuals and families in southeastern West Virginia to achieve self-sufficiency and improve their quality of life.

Community Action of South Eastern West Virginia, Inc. (CASEWV) has been serving the people of southeastern West Virginia since its charter in 1964. As the third such agency established in West Virginia, CASEWV is dedicated to empowering individuals and families to achieve self-sufficiency and improve their overall quality of life.

Driven by a volunteer Board of Directors representing a diverse cross-section of the community—elected officials, private sector leaders, and representation of the limited-income sector—CASEWV ensures that the voices of those it serves are heard and their needs are met. The organization envisions safe, healthy, inclusive communities to support individuals and families in reaching their full potential.
